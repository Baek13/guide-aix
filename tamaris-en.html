My favorites

Local Recommendations

Make the most of your stay by discovering my favorite spots in Aix-en-Provence!

🍽️ Restaurants

  • Sin Ky: An excellent Vietnamese restaurant.
  • Da Noemi: A very popular little Italian place.
  • Le Four Aixois: A safe bet for great pizzas.
  • Le Galifet: A wonderful address for lunch. The setting is superb, and prices are more accessible at lunchtime than in the evening.
  • Rue Boulegon: Take a stroll down this street where you'll find several very pleasant restaurants and terraces.

☕ Drinks & Sweets

  • Le Vieux Tonneau: A lovely spot for a drink with a light bite, mostly frequented by locals.
  • Brasserie du Grillon: Not to be missed! Have a drink on the Cours Mirabeau at this true local institution with very reasonable prices.
  • Béchard: While on the Cours Mirabeau, make a stop at this famous pastry shop.
  • Hôtel de Caumont: We also recommend having a drink here; its garden is particularly charming and offers a beautiful setting.

🧺 The Markets

If you are in Aix in the morning, the markets are a big part of the city's charm:

  • Provençal Market: (Fruits, vegetables, crafts, fabrics) Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday mornings on the Place de Verdun and city center streets.
  • Place Richelme: A food-focused market held every morning.
  • Clothing Market: Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday mornings on the Cours Mirabeau.
  • Flower Market: Held on Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday mornings on the Place de l'Hôtel de Ville, with colorful stalls and a typical Provençal atmosphere.
